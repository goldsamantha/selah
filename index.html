Selah

She believes first and foremost in herself. Selah is 17 years old, captain of the Spirit Squad, most likely candidate for class valedictorian, and at the heart of it: ruthlessly selfish and as cruel as circumstances call for with little regard for consequences.

She’s never made a habit out of feeling guilty.
